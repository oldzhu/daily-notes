Why It Hung

python -m debugpy --listen ... --wait-for-client starts a DAP server that blocks until a debugger performs the full handshake.
Your client script stopped after initialize, so debugpy never saw the mandatory attach followed by configurationDone; it kept waiting, hence the hang.

Takeaway

Debugpy server mode works fine locally; just ensure your client sends attach and configurationDone after initialize.
I’ll mirror this sequence inside our backend so /use python no longer times out.
