How to use the new shared-folder option

Regenerate run_qemu.sh in your bundle: .[syzbot_prepare_qemu_repro.py](http://_vscodecontentref_/2) --extid a9528028ab4ca83e8bac --force
Boot with share enabled:
cd repro/a9528028ab4ca83e8bac
SHARE_DIR=$PWD SHARE_MOUNT=/mnt/host ./run_qemu.sh
In the guest:
mkdir -p /mnt/host
mount -t 9p -o trans=virtio,version=9p2000.L hostshare /mnt/host
